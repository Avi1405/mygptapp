FAQ: World Cup 2026 schedule and kickoff times

What time is my team playing?

Use the country filter to pick your team. Every fixture is converted from UTC into your local clock so you can see kickoff time instantly.

How can I filter 2026 World Cup matches by country?

Open the country dropdown and choose any nation. Matches are included when that country appears as either the home or away team.

Where is each World Cup match being played?

Every match card shows the stage, stadium name, and host city so you can quickly see venue details for each fixture.
